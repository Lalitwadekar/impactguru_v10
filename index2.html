I held my baby boy in my arms. My husband and I named him Devansh, meaning a part of God’. My beautiful baby boy’s grace was no less than that of God. It meant the world to me when he held my finger with his tiny little hands.

Fate only took 5 days to tear it all apart. Right when we were ready to take him home with us, we took him for his necessary vaccination. That is when the mishap took place.

The injection accidentally pierced my son’s bone and caused the infection. The infection quickly spread to his lungs and led to the development of pneumonia. He is also suffering Pathological fracture of the left femur and extubation failure with seizures.

In just a blink of an eye, my healthy baby went from my arms to the ventilator.

His days of playing with toys are now replaced by pipes and needles that are keeping him alive.

Now I cannot even touch him. I don’t know what to do anymore. My happiness was stolen by my fate in a quick second.

The doctors at KIMS Cuddles Hospital have advised below mentioned treatments to save my son:-

  • Ventilator support
  • Higher antibiotics
  • Inj. Heparin
  • Blood pressure medications
  • PICU stay for 7-10 days and the ward stay for 10 days

These medications and treatments can save my 2 m/o baby boy. These are very high-end treatments.

My husband works in a mobile shop for an income of Rs. 10,000 per month. We have tried everything and yet we are unable to arrange such a huge amount of money. My husband only has me and our kid. We have no other family support. 

We have very little time left. Will you please help me and my husband? With your timely support, we can save our baby and finally take him home. 

Please help me hold my baby again.
